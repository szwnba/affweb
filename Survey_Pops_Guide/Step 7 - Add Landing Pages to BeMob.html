After you've created your landing page(s), upload them to your hosting account and test it in your browser. If it works as you expect then grab the URL(s); we'll add it to BeMob so you can use it in your campaigns.

In your BeMob account:
  1. Navigate to the Landings tab.
  2. Click + New.


In the modal that pops up:
  1. Fill in a Name.
  2. Paste your landing page URL.
  3. Click Save.


Your landing page is now added to BeMob!
