Step 5 - Setting Up Zeydoo Survey Offers in BeMob


Now we are going to set up the Zeydoo Survey Offers in BeMob.

Log into your Zeydoo account:
  1. On the offers page, fill in the Vertical box with Dating Survey, Betting Survey, Finance Survey, and Sweeps Survey.


UPDATE July 14th, 2022: Zeydoo has updated their interface and the survey offers can now all be found on their own dedicated page. Just click Smart Surveys in the left sidebar and you should see all of the survey offers. Filtering the offers by vertical like shown originally will not show you any offers.



This will filter the offers to show you only the Zeydoo Survey Offers that we'll be using in this guide.

About the Survey Offers​

You're welcome to try any and/or all of the survey offers (dating, betting, finance, or sweeps). However, make sure you take note of the targeting requirements for the offer you select.

The dating and betting surveys at the moment are only available for Facebook, Samsung, and Chrome browsers on Android devices. There's only one offer for each of those surveys.

While on the other hand, the Finance Survey has multiple offers available, which are divided by device types and browsers (Android, iOS, Mac, Windows, etc.).

Recently Zeydoo also added a SMART Finance Survey (may not appear in your account), which is supposed to send the users to the correct Finance Survey offer if the user matches the targeting requirements of one of the available offers.

I'll use the SMART Finance Survey as an example to show you how to pull the links you need since it's new and I haven't yet set up links in my account for it.

At the bottom of the offer page click Request approval.



Fill out the modal and then click Request approval. Wait until it's approved.



Once approved you can create your offer URLs below the offer's description. Click the blue Start working with this offer button.



Make 1 link for each available landing page on the survey offer you selected.



For the traffic source name you can put whatever you want (I used 1 and 2). I'd suggest not using the real name of the source you're using for your own privacy.

Next, take each of the links for your offer and head to your BeMob dashboard.
  1. In the main menu, click Offers.
  2. Click + New.


In the modal that pops up, you can fill out your offer details. Make sure you replace the [SOURCE_ID] and [CLICK_ID] tokens in the URL with the BeMob tokens:



Click Save.

Once you've added each of the offer URLs for the offer you selected offer you're ready to add some landing pages.
