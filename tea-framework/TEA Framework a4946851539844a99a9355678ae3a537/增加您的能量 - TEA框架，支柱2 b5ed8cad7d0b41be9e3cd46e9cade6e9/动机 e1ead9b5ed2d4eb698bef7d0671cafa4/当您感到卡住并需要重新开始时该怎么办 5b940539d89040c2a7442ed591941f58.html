当您感到卡住并需要重新开始时该怎么办

https://www.asianefficiency.com/podcasts/148-reset/

Have you ever felt stressed from being overcommitted but didn’t know how to fix the situation? You need someone who can show you how to break out of the mundane and make sure you’re moving in the right direction towards creating your ideal future. If so, you’re not alone. Many people feel completely overwhelmed with everything they need to do, but they don’t know how to start over or slow down. In this episode, Brooks and Mike talk about what to do when you find yourself in this situation and you need to make a fresh start. If you’ve ever felt stuck, this episode has practical tips and strategies to help you get out of your rut and hit the reset button.
