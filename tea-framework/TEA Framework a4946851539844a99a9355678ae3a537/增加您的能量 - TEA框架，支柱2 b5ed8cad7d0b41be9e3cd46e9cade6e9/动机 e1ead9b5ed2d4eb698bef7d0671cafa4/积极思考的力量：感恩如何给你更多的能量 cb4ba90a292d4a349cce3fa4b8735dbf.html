积极思考的力量：感恩如何给你更多的能量

https://www.asianefficiency.com/podcasts/175-positive-energy/

Ever find yourself with time to work on your important project but you don’t still don’t get anything done because you just don’t have the energy to work on it? In this episode, Brooks and Mike explain why energy management (and not time management) is the key to taking massive action on your goals. We explore the power of gratitude, showing you how a simple mindset shift can help you become happier AND more productive. We talk about why living your life according to the old saying that “life is a marathon” is actually a very bad idea if you want to achieve your goals, teach you the mindset you should be developing instead, and share some of our best tips for staying positive. If you’re ready to go beyond basic time management and start making serious progress towards your ideal future, this episode is for you.
